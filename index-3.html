With over three hundred 5-Star reviews... we're a top-rated unisex hair and beauty spa in Purley.

Welcome to Abi's Hair and Beauty Spa, Purley's premier Aveda salon specializing in Afro and European hair. With over 23 years of expertise, we offer a wide range of luxury services, including precision cuts, vibrant coloring, relaxing treatments, weave-ons, hair extensions, facials, and more.
